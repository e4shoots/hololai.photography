About

Hololai was started by two high school students, Elia Bernaldes and Sage Olores, with one goal in mind—capturing moments in a way that actually means something. We live and operate on the island of Kauaʻi, where we’re surrounded by natural beauty, culture, and real stories every day. Growing up here has shaped how we see photography—not just as taking pictures, but as preserving the feeling behind them. Our mission is simple: to capture moments that matter in a way that feels honest, personal, and timeless. Whether it’s a big milestone or a small, meaningful moment, we want every photo to take you right back to how it felt. Hololai isn’t a big company—it’s just us, putting everything we have into our work. We care about the people we shoot and the stories we tell, making sure every image feels real—because at the end of the day, it’s not just about photos, it’s about memories you’ll actually want to keep.
